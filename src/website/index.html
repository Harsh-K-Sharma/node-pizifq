We have used the same Rockets to build Tinker. And these are some of the best in the world. With these Rockets you are getting the transfer speeds of upto 1000 km/hr, with the options to not only choose but change the rocket at any time.

With Tinker you can choose to fly with a Use & Throw Rocket or a Resuable Rocket with no maintainence cost or damages.

You pay for only what you fly & how long you fly. Nothing more !
